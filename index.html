Frequently Asked Questions

Do you provide food during treks?

Yes, we provide meals, snacks, and drinking water on all treks, except the free Sunday trek in Jodhpur (as it’s only 3–4 hours long). For that, please carry your own water bottle.

Where do Sunday treks start in Jodhpur?

The starting point is announced on our Instagram stories every week. Make sure to check them a day before and arrive on time.

What should I carry on a trek?

Carry a small backpack with water, sports shoes, a cap/hat, sunscreen, and personal medicines. For multi-day treks, a detailed packing list will be shared.

How do I book a trek?

You can book a trek by clicking on the Book Now button under each trip. You’ll be redirected to our Instagram DM or WhatsApp to confirm your slot.

Are treks safe for beginners?

Yes, most of our single-day treks in Jodhpur (like Goram Ghat, Bheel Beri Waterfall, Jawai) are beginner-friendly. Our guides ensure safety and support throughout.

Do you arrange transport?

Yes, transport is arranged for longer treks like Kedarkantha, Kedarnath, and Har Ki Dun. For local Jodhpur treks, transport is usually not included but can be managed on request.
